A little about us…

We're always looking for more players for our social mixed touch rugby group. There's no need for prior experience as all rules and skills can be taught whilst playing.

Past and present players have played in a wide variety of other leagues, venues and levels. So it's a great opportunity for beginners to benefit from more experienced players' knowledge. And also for more experienced players to help beginners develop their skills and learn the game.

The touch pitches are located on the Streatham side of Tooting Bec Common, between the train tracks. The nearest postcode is SW16 1AD and the nearest stations are Balham or Streatham Hill. We play on a full size marked out Touch Rugby pitch. As the pitch is hired there is a collection of £3 per person per session. We’ll never ask you to subscribe for a season or year; it’s pay and play.

There are no fixed teams and there is no commitment needed. Just turn up when you're free and leave when you're tired. We do play most touch rules, with less emphasis on penalties, but tailored to the individual and their experience playing touch.

To register, join our Facebook Group. We look forward to seeing on the pitch on Saturday!
